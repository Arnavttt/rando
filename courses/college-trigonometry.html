Use for:
SAS
(two sides + included angle),
SSS
(all three sides)
Solving for angle: cosC = (a² + b² − c²)/(2ab)
Area: K = ½ab sinC
Solve triangle: a=8, b=10, C=60° (SAS — use Law of Cosines)
1
c² = a² + b² − 2ab cosC = 64 + 100 − 2(8)(10)cos60°
2

3

4

<div class="callout"><div class="callout-lbl">Law of Sines: a/sinA = b/sinB = c/sinC</div><ul><li>Use for: <strong>AAS, ASA</strong> (two angles + side), <strong>SSA</strong> (ambiguous case)</li><li><strong>Ambiguous case (SSA):</strong> May have 0, 1, or 2 solutions — always check!</li></ul></div>
