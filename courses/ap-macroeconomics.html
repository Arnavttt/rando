AP Macroeconomics

A complete AP Macroeconomics path from scarcity through indicators, aggregate models, fiscal/monetary policy, long-run growth, and open-economy international finance. Emphasis: models, graphs, policy chains, and FRQ precision.

27lessons

Start here

Status: New AP course added in the same chunked interactive format.

Suggested pacing: One semester or full-year path; pair graph practice with short written chains of causation.

Official course/reference page

Prerequisites

  • No formal prerequisite, but algebra and graph interpretation help.
  • Students should practice cause/effect sequences and policy reasoning.
  • Memorize graph axes, curve names, shifts, and equilibrium outcomes.

Course skills

  • Define macroeconomic principles and models.
  • Interpret economic outcomes and indicators.
  • Determine outcomes of policy and economic shocks.
  • Model situations using graphs and visuals.
