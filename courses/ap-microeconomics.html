SPLIT COURSE PATH · NEW AP COURSE

AP Microeconomics

A complete AP Microeconomics path from scarcity and decision-making through markets, firms, imperfect competition, factor markets, market failure, and government policy. Emphasis: graphs, marginal reasoning, incentives, efficiency, and FRQ explanation.

26lessons

Start here

Status: New AP course added in the same chunked interactive format.

Suggested pacing: One semester or full-year path; draw and explain graphs constantly, then practice FRQs after every unit.

Official course/reference page

Prerequisites

  • No formal prerequisite, but algebra and graph interpretation help.
  • Students should be comfortable explaining cause/effect and tradeoffs.
  • Practice labeling axes, curves, equilibrium, and welfare areas.

Course skills

  • Define economic principles and models.
  • Interpret economic outcomes.
  • Determine outcomes of specific situations.
  • Model economic situations using graphs and visuals.
