SPLIT COURSE PATH · NEW AP COURSE

AP Comparative Government and Politics

A comparative politics path focused on China, Iran, Mexico, Nigeria, Russia, and the United Kingdom. Students learn concepts, country evidence, and comparison skills needed for concept application, quantitative analysis, comparative analysis, and argument FRQs.

22lessons

Start here

Status: New AP course added in the same chunked interactive format.

Suggested pacing: Semester or full-year path; build country comparison charts while learning each concept.

Official course/reference page

Prerequisites

  • No formal prerequisite.
  • Students should be willing to compare countries rather than memorize isolated facts.
  • Basic current-events awareness helps but is not required.

Course skills

  • Compare political concepts, systems, institutions, policies, and processes.
  • Use country-specific evidence from all six course countries.
  • Analyze data, political concepts, and political changes.
  • Write clear FRQ claims supported by evidence and reasoning.
