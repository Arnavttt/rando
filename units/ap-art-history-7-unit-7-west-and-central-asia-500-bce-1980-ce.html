Unit 7 · Lesson 1

Persian, Islamic, and Central Asian artistic traditions

Persian, Islamic, and Central Asian artistic traditions should be studied through form, function, content, context, materials, technique, audience, patronage, and cultural meaning. Always practice describing visible evidence before making an interpretation.
