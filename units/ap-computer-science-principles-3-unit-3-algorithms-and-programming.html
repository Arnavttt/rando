Unit 3 · Lesson 1
Learn on this site

AP pseudocode conventions and written response vocabulary should be learned by tracing examples and then building your own version. Know the vocabulary, the syntax or pseudocode pattern, what data changes at each step, and the edge cases that could break the program.
