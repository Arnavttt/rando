Music Fundamentals III — Lesson 1: melodic organization and motives; phrase structure and antecedent/consequent

Unit 3 · Lesson 1
Learn on this site

Music theory must be read, heard, written, and analyzed. For every concept, drill the symbol, sound, written notation, and harmonic function.

melodic organization and motives

melodic organization and motives must be understood visually, aurally, and functionally. Be able to notate it, hear it, sing/play it, identify it in an excerpt, and explain how it functions in a musical context.

Test yourself: teach this idea from memory, give one example, identify one trap, and answer one exam-style question about it.
