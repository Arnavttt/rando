Unit 2 · Lesson 5
Learn on this site

Do not only memorize definitions. Trace execution, predict outputs, explain why the program behaves that way, and then write a smaller version yourself.

privacy, personally identifiable information, and anonymization should be learned by tracing examples and then building your own version. Know the vocabulary, the syntax or pseudocode pattern, what data changes at each step, and the edge cases that could break the program.
