Longer Fiction or Drama I — Lesson 1: character arcs and relationships; conflict over time

Unit 3 · Lesson 1
Learn on this site

The core question is never “what happened?” but “how do choices create meaning, argument, tone, structure, or effect for a reader/audience?”

character arcs and relationships

character arcs and relationships is a reading-and-writing skill. Learn what to notice in a text, how to name the author’s or writer’s choice, and how to explain the effect of that choice on meaning, argument, audience, tone, or structure.
