Short Fiction III — Lesson 1: experimental structure and fragmentation; stream of consciousness/interior monologue

Unit 7 · Lesson 1
Learn on this site

The core question is never “what happened?” but “how do choices create meaning, argument, tone, structure, or effect for a reader/audience?”

experimental structure and fragmentation

experimental structure and fragmentation is a reading-and-writing skill. Learn what to notice in a text, how to name the author’s or writer’s choice, and how to explain the effect of that choice on meaning, argument, audience, tone, or structure.
