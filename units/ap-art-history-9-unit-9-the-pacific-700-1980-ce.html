The Pacific, 700–1980 CE — Lesson 1: Oceania and Pacific Island cultures; navigation, genealogy, ritual, status, exchange

Unit 9 · Lesson 1
Learn on this site

For every work/tradition, connect form, function, content, context, material, technique, patron/audience, and why the work mattered in its culture.

Oceania and Pacific Island cultures

Oceania and Pacific Island cultures should be studied through form, function, content, context, materials, technique, audience, patronage, and cultural meaning. Always practice describing visible evidence before making an interpretation.
