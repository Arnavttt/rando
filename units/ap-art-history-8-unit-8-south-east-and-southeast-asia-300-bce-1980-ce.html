Unit 8 · Lesson 1
Learn on this site

Hindu, Buddhist, Jain, Confucian, Daoist, Shinto contexts

Hindu, Buddhist, Jain, Confucian, Daoist, Shinto contexts should be studied through form, function, content, context, materials, technique, audience, patronage, and cultural meaning. Always practice describing visible evidence before making an interpretation.
